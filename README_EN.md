## ğŸ’¡ Tips
### For Better Photos
1. **Shoot in Bright Places**: Recommended to shoot in bright locations due to reduced colors
2. **Be Conscious of Contrast**: Subjects with clear color contrasts look great
3. **Simple Composition**: Simple compositions are more effective than complex backgrounds
4. **Shoot Close**: Small subjects can get crushed at low resolution, so shoot them large
5. **Experiment**: Try different settings to discover interesting expressions
### Comfortable Shooting with Hardware Buttons
- **Volume Buttons**: Easy one-handed shooting
- **Bluetooth Shutter**: Convenient for selfies and group photos
- Hardware buttons save directly, skipping the preview
